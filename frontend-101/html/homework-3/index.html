***Fried Mussels Recipe***

Fried mussels are a delicious food that you can easily cook at your home...


Ingredients

  • 500 Grams of mussel meat
  • 2 Cups of flour
  • 250ml Seltzer water
  • 200 ml water
  • 1 Dessert spoon of baking soda
  • 1 Teaspon of salt

Instructions

  1. Pour the flour, seltzer water, water, baking soda and salt into a large bowl and whisk well.
  2. Add mussels to the this dressing and mix a little bit and slowly.
  3. Then wait for mussels to soak this dressing for 4 hours minimum.
  4. Put mussels on skewers.
  5. Add sunflower oil to the pan and wait for it to heat up.
  6. Cook until the color of the mussels turn between golden yellow and brown

Bon Apetite !

For 4 People

Preparation Time: 4 hours and 30 mins

Cooking Time: 30 Mins
